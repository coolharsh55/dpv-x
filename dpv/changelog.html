[v0.5] - 02 April 2022

v0.5 makes changes to the structuring of DPV, with two new modules for expressing Entities and Risk related concepts. It also adds the DPV-LEGAL extension providing a taxonomy of jurisdictions, laws, and authorities.

Risk:The existing concepts from Technical and Organisational Measures related to risk were consolidated under the Risk module alongside additional concepts associated with consequences and impacts.

Entities: The earlier entities module has been restructured to differentiate between different categorisations of entities, such as legal roles, basic concepts, types of organisations, authorities, etc.

[v0.4] - 15 February 2022

v0.4 makes important and backwards incompatible changes in terms of how it expresses the concepts and relationships. It also moves the namespace to w3id.org for all its documents. Most importantly, DPV is now presented as an abstract 'data model' that is recorded using SKOS. Specific serialisations are provided for use as an ontology in two variants: first using SKOS+RDFS and the second using OWL2. In addition to these, the personal data concepts have been moved to a separate extension, i.e. DPV-PD, to prevent overlap and conflict with other concepts still in DPV. A Primer has been added to provide an overview of the DPV, its semantics, and concepts, and also to explain the above changes in structure.

[v0.3] - 01 Oct 2021

Release Notes

v0.3 introduces changes in purposes and legal basis as a new module, and makes some backwards-incompatible changes through changes in terms and removal of unclear/ambiguous concepts

The purposes module has terms - added: 27 ; removed: 5 ; changed: 9. Details:

  • SellTargettedAdvertisements: Removed since Advertisement is covered under another concept. If this is meant to sell advertisements as a product, a combination of SellProducts and Advertising should be used.
  • EnforceSecurity: Renamed from Security to express its use as a purpose and clarify separation from technical measures.
  • CommercialInterest: Removed due to lack of clarity on what 'commercial' means, and more importantly in expressing its application as a purpose.
  • EnforceAccessControl: Renamed from AccessControl for similar reason to Security.
  • Personalisation: Specific category of purposes to express personalisation as an objective in itself or for another purpose.
  • RecordManagement: Added as a purpose along with subclass for ServiceRecordManagement
  • Concepts for vendor management within organisation: VendorSelectionAssessment, VendorRecordsManagement, VendorPayment, VendorManagement
  • Concepts for customer management within organisation: CustomerManagement, CustomerOrderManagement, CustomerSolvencyMonitoring, CustomerRelationshipManagement, CustomerClaimsManagement
  • Concepts for other organisational processes: PublicRelations, AccountManagement, OrganisationComplianceManagement, HumanResourceManagement, OrganisationRiskManagement, OrganisationGovernance, DisputeManagement
  • TechnicalServiceProvision: Added to be specific about service provision involving technical implementation.
  • SellProducts: Added as indicative of a broad category of selling products and services, with subclasses to indicate whom the product is being sold to - SellDataToThirdParties, SellInsightsFromData, SellProductsToDataSubject
  • RequestedServiceProvision: Specific purpose to indicate the service provision is requested by the consumer.
  • MemberPartnerManagement: Broad concept for expressing the purposes associated with managing members or partners in an organisation e.g. clubs
  • ServiceUsageAnalytics: Renamed from UsageAnalytics to be more specific about what is being analysed.
  • CommunicationManagement: Indicative broad purpose expressing requirements associated with communication of information or action.
  • Marketing: Added term as a broad category of activities associated with marketing and advertising.
  • ServiceOptimization: Changed as Subclass of ServiceProvision since optimisation is usually considered part of providing services.
  • ServicePersonalization: Subclass of ServiceProvision since personalisation is usually considered part of providing services.
  • ServiceUsageAnalytics: Changed from UsageAnalytics for less ambiguity and more preciseness.

The processing module has terms - added: 0 ; removed: 0 ; changed: 1. The property hasConsequence has been renamed to remove the plural from hasConsequences.

The technical_organisational_measures module has terms - added: 12 ; removed: 1 ; changed: 1. Details:

  • ContractualTerms: Changed from Contract to distinguish between organisational measure and legal basis.
  • Added concepts regarding documents and documentation: Notice, DataProcessingRecords, RecordsOfActivities, Policy, RegisterOfProcessingActivities, PrivacyNotice
  • Concepts for assessments of various kinds: Assessment, LegitimateInterestAssessment, DataTransferImpactAssessment
  • Concepts associated with data transfers: SafeguardForDataTransfer, Safeguard

The entities module has terms - added: 2 ; removed: 0 ; changed: 0. Two concepts were added in relevance with data transfers and the role of actors therein - DataImporter, DataExporter.

The legal_basis module has terms - added: 14 ; removed: 0 ; changed: 0. This represents expanding the taxonomy to provide various kinds of legal basis available at a broad level. These are:

  • Contract and its sub-classes EnterIntoContract and ContractPerformance
  • LegitimateInterest further clarified as LegitimateInterestOfController and LegitimateInterestOfThirdParty
  • OfficialAuthorityOfController
  • Consent
  • VitalInterest further clarified as VitalInterestOfDataSubject and VitalInterestOfNaturalPerson
  • PublicInterest
  • LegalObligation
  • DataTransferLegalBasis as a special category for explicitly indicating data basis for transfers of personal data

[v0.2] - 14 Jan 2021

Release Notes

v0.2 represents an update to v0.1 in terms of added new concepts in the hierarchy, expanded base vocabulary, and some backwards-incompatible changes.

The core/base vocabulary has new concepts for Risk, Right, and DataSubjectRight. These are associated with PersonalDataHandling using the relations hasRisk and hasRight respectively.

Personal Data Categories has new concepts for BrowsingReferral, PaymentCard, PaymentCardNumber, PaymentCardExpiry, DeviceSoftware, DeviceOperatingSystem, DeviceApplications, AuthenticationHistory

Purposes has new concepts for Marketing, DirectMarketing, Advertising, PersonalisedAdvertising, UsageAnalytics, CommunicationForCustomerCare, LegalCompliance, Payment, SocialMediaMarketing, RegistrationAuthentication

Processing attributes, such as those for indicating systematic monitoring, large scale, etc. have been removed as boolean properties and added as classes. Full list: SystematicMonitoring, EvaluationScoring, MatchingCombining, AutomatedDecisionMaking, LargeScaleProcessing, InnovativeUseOfNewTechnologies. This is so as to enable assocaiting additional information and semantics for concepts, such as specific types, categories, relations, and enable broader use in context of processing. For example, indicating the specific algorithm or process for automated decision making - which could not be indicated using a boolean value. In addition to these, the concept for DataSource has been added. Generic properties added are: hasDataSource, hasAlgorithmicLogic, hasConsequences, hasHumanInvolvement.

Technical and Organisational Measures have new concepts for: SingleSignOn, ImpactAssessment, PIA, DPIA, Consultation, ConsultationWithAuthority. The concept RiskMitigationMeasure and its associated property mitigatesRisk has been added to indicate risk and mitigation. The existing properties for storage, location, duration, and measure implementation have been modified to avoid name duplication. For example, storage as a property and Storage as a concept are equivalent when used as HTML identifiers. To avoid this, the names have been prefixed, which also adds consistency to the vocabulary naming. The properties are: measureImplementedBy, hasStorage, hasLocation, hasDuration.

Entities added as new concepts include LegalEntity, DataSubProcessor, Child, DataProtectionOfficer, Representative, Authority, DataProtectionAuthority, VulnerableDataSubject. Generic properties for expressing information about entities added are: hasName, hasAddress, hasContact, hasIdentifier, hasRepresentative.

[v0.1] - 19 Nov 2020

Release Notes

v0.1 consists of the first release in 18-JUN-2019, and includes subsequent updates till the last update on 19-NOV-2020. This version introduced the 'core' concepts of DPV, or the base vocabulary, and a taxonomical hierarchy of concepts expanding the vocabulary.

The various changes to the vocabulary have all been consolidated under a single version, namely v0.1. Moving forward, the vocabulary will utilise semantic versioning of the form MAJOR.MINOR.TRIVIAL. The v0.x notation denotes the ongoing progress towards a stable and mature vocabulary which will be finalised as v1.
